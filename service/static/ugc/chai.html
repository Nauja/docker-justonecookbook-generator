Masala Chai With Fennel Seeds

Course: Drink
Cuisine: Indian
Keyword: masala, chai, spicy, cinnamon
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 1 cup
Learn how to make Masala Chai, a traditional Indian drink. This version contains a mix of black peppercorns, cloves, star anise, cinnamon and fennel seeds for a lovely spiciness.

Ingredients

  • 4 black peppercorns
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • tsp fennel seeds
  • 200 ml milk (Can use vegetal milk)
  • 1 tbsp sugar (Helps improve spiciness)
  • 1 tbsp black tea (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse a small saucepan with cold water.

  2. Add together the milk, the spices and the sugar into the saucepan, cover and let simmer for 5 minutes over medium/high heat.

  3. Add the black tea into the saucepan, cover and let simmer for another 5 minutes over low/medium heat.

  4. Filter directly into the cup, then let cool down for 5 minutes as its quite hot.

  5. Remove the milk skin that formed on top if any. You can now enjoy your Masala Chai !

Recipe Notes

Rinsing the saucepan with cold water and covering the saucepan are extra steps to help prevent formation of milk skin.

You can skip adding black tea into the saucepan for a more kid friendly version or to drink it at night.
